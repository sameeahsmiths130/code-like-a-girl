    • Rabbits are small mammals with fluffy, short tails, whiskers and distinctive long ears. Happy rabbits practice a cute behavior known as a “binky:” they jump up in the air and twist and spin around!

Fish tanks also add a touch of tranquility to your home. ... The best fish for kids or beginners include goldfish, tetra, betta fish, danios and guppies
